My Top Favorite Movies

By Leilani Price

The Lord of the Rings

I love this movie because it's Tolkien. I could really get into a lot of comments
about the flaws and errors, and to be honest, I couldn't even finish The Hobbit
but I still absolutely love the LotR trilogy. Minus the guy who plays Frodo, who drives me nuts.

Um. The Princess Bride

Delightful, fun, hits all the proper elements, something for everyone and
full of eminently quotable sayings!

Been too long! But I remember liking it.
